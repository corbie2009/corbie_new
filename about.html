About CoRBiE

Rather than breaking down after reading the poor wildlife in the country few energetic young man joined hands to work something for betterment of the wildlife and the formed Conservation & Research of Bio-Diversity and Environment (CORBIE). CORBIE is the biological name of CROW, just as the crow cleans the dirt and junk of the society CORBIE also promises to itself to clean the dirt of the society by lighting up the minds of people by spreading the knowledge about how Bio-Diversity is essential for all of us and how to preserve it. Main goal of CORBIE is to preserve the wild life of India by enlarging the forested area and by working in every sphere of it.

The Origin

CORBIE is a non-government organization (NGO) founded in the year of 2009 by a bunch of service minded and dedicated group of people with a mission to work for the uplift of the environment and to address unmet needs in the community. CORBIE is registered under the society registration act, 1860 since the year of 2014.

 

The Mission

To preserve the forest areas of India and to work for uplift of the wildlife by taking part in right direction to achieve the same so as to achieve the harmony among the diverse life on earth.

 

The Vision

We have joined hands to work towards preservation of wildlife in particular and Bio-Diversity in general and we urge one and all to join us and to help own.

 

The Goal

  1. To initiate, carry out, execute, implement, aid and assist activities towards conservation of Bio-Diversity and Environment in every aspect.
  2. To carry out training programs for one and all to teach about the importance of wildlife and environment in human life.
  3. To organize seminars, road shows to promote good habits towards the environment.
  4. To promote use of environment friendly articles by people.
  5. To organize educational institutes training for the students towards conservation of Bio-Diversity.
  6. To do forestation.
  7. To do activities to preserve and enlarge the forested areas in the country.
  8. To take programs to create awareness about Disaster Management and its relation with Bio-Diversity and Environment.
  9. To work for Central as well as different State Governments at both policy formulation and implementation level with regards to Bio-Diversity and Environment.
  10. To encourage the habit of nature love among the people.
  11. To create awareness among the people about how the wild life is an integral part of our life.
  12. To work in the field of reproduction of the wild and domestic animals whose species are in danger in particular as well as for all the species found.
  13. To publish journals, papers, books, magazines to promote and educate the people about the Bio-Diversity and Environment.
  14. To promote participation of youths in the field of Bio-Diversity and Environment.
  15. To create employment in this field to bring human and their Bio-Diversity closer.
  16. To promote the use of information and Communication Technology for socio environmental development.
  17. To encourage sustainable development by guiding the developer with Environmental Impact Assessment.
  18. To formulate ground based Environmental Management Plan for the developer to ensure ecological balance.

 

Location / Area of Operation

CORBIE is presently working actively in several districts of Assam but dedicated to work at any part of the country as well as to work in foreign location in joint collaboration in near future.
