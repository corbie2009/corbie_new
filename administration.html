Message from the desk of CoRBiE

Posted By Admin    on April 12, 2020    Comments (0)

CoRBiE was convinced in the 2009 out of my love wild animals and forest. CoRBiE aims to work of conservation of wildlife & forest as well as to act against illegal wildlife trade.CORBIE has been also working to release the pet birds, that are being kept in many households, into their natural habitats. Awareness programmes cum workshops regarding Wildlife Protection Act, are organised regularly among different stakeholders from time to time. Its been 10 years of journey working in the field of conservation and the journey will continue.Still a long way to go towards achieving our aims & objectives. I appeal to each and everyone to support this cause and make it a success.
