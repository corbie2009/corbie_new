Green Earth campaign (Sapling plantation)

Forests play an important role in climate change. The destruction and degradation of forests contributes to the problem through the release of CO2. But the planting of new forests can help mitigate against climate change.

We started this from cleaning Pagladia River. In future we are going to act for mitigation of overall environmental pollution and reduce wastages by recycling using cutting age technologies.
