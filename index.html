CoRBiE

CoRBiE was convinced in the 2009 out of my love wild animals and forest. CoRBiE aims to work of conservation of wildlife & forest as well as to act against illegal wildlife trade.CORBIE has been also working to release the pet birds, that are being kept in many households, into their natural habitats. Awareness programmes cum workshops regarding Wildlife Protection Act, are organised regularly among different stakeholders from time to time. Its been 10 years of journey.....

Conservation & Research of Bio-Diversity & Environment (CoRBiE)

CoRBiE-What's Behind

Rather than breaking down after reading the poor wildlife in the country few energetic young man joined hands to work something for betterment of the wildlife and the formed Conservation & Research of Bio-Diversity and Environment (CORBIE). CORBIE is the biological name of CROW, just as the crow cleans the dirt and junk of the society CORBIE also promises to itself to clean the dirt of the society by lighting up the minds of people by spreading the knowledge about how Bio-Diversity is essential for all of us and how to preserve it. Main goal of CORBIE is to preserve the wild life of India by enlarging the forested area and by working in every sphere of it.
